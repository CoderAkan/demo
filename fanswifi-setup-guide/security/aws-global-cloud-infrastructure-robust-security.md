# AWS: Global Cloud Infrastructure & Robust Security

![](../../.gitbook/assets/aws-global-cloud-infrastructure-robust-security-365.png)

FansWiFi takes your security seriously, and that's why we have partnered with Amazon Web Services (AWS) to provide you with a reliable and secure service. Here are the key benefits of using FansWiFi with AWS:

## Reliable Service and World-class Hosting Infrastructure

Our partnership with AWS ensures that your data is always available whenever you need it. AWS has a proven track record of delivering reliable services and maintaining a world-class hosting infrastructure.

## Compliance with ISO 27001 & SOC Certifications

AWS holds certifications for compliance with ISO 27001 and SOC, demonstrating their commitment to security and industry best practices. This means that your data is stored and processed in a secure environment that meets globally recognized security standards.

## Strengthened Security Posture and Access to Tools

By leveraging AWS, we enhance our overall security posture. AWS provides us with access to a wide range of security tools and services that help us meet specific security assurance requirements more efficiently. This allows us to reduce costs and save time while ensuring the highest level of security for your data.

## Proactive Monitoring and Issue Resolution

AWS services are designed to keep a close eye on any changes or security issues in the system. This proactive monitoring helps us identify and resolve potential problems before they can impact your experience. With AWS, we can quickly respond to any security incidents and handle a large volume of work efficiently.

## Top-notch Security Measures for Cloud Infrastructure

AWS is renowned for its top-notch security measures. Their global cloud infrastructure is built with robust security technology and controls, providing a secure foundation for building, migrating, and managing applications and workloads. With FansWiFi and AWS, you can trust that your data is protected with the highest level of security.

## Empowering Innovation with Comprehensive Security Services

Through our integration with AWS, we can leverage a comprehensive range of security services and partners. This empowers our business to innovate securely while maintaining the highest level of protection for your data. We are committed to staying at the forefront of security technology to ensure your peace of mind.
