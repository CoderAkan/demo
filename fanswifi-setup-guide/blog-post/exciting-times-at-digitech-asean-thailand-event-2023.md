# Exciting times at DigiTech ASEAN Thailand event 2023!

![](../../.gitbook/assets/exciting-times-at-digitech-asean-thailand-event-2023-454.png)

We had an amazing time at DigiTech ASEAN Thailand! 

Surrounded by tech enthusiasts and innovators from the ASEAN region, the event provided a fantastic opportunity to showcase FansWiFis Social WiFi Marketing solutions and connect with like-minded individuals who share our passion for technology.

A big thank you to the organizers - [DigiTech ASEAN Thailand](https://digitechasean.com/) for bringing together a diverse range of exhibitors. This event allowed us to connect with the market, reach new customers and partners, meet decision-makers, and enhance our brand awareness in this competitive but growing market.

We're grateful to all the visitors who stopped by our booth.  We appreciate the engaging conversations, valuable feedback, and the opportunity to showcase how our solutions benefit your businesses. Thank you for your support, and we look forward to continuing our partnership in the future.
