## Q: What is the difference between the SMEs and Pro plan?

**A:** The Pro plan is equipped with advanced marketing tools, including survey campaigns and CRM integration capabilities, to help elevate your business's social WiFi strategy. Additionally, the Pro plan allows up to 5 users to be administrators with role-based access, offering greater flexibility compared to the 2-admin limit of the standard SME plan.

## Q: Is an additional fee incurred if I use more than one AP hardware device?

**A:** Yes, there is an additional fee for each WiFi access point beyond the first one, with pricing dependent on the selected plan. Please refer to [this page](https://www.fanswifi.com/product) for the specific pricings.

## Q: Is there a free trial available?

**A:** Yes, both the SMEs and Pro plans come with a 7-day free trial. During the trial, you can access all of our tools at no cost. After the 7-day trial, you will be billed annually based on the plan you choose.

## Q: Are AP hardware devices included in my plan?

**A:** No, the AP hardware devices are not included in the SMEs and Pro plan pricing. However, you can add on our pre-configured FansWiFi AP devices when selecting a plan.

## Q: What features can I add to my plan?

**A:** You can add the redeemable coupon, point rewards, and e-stamp features to your plan. Please contact us to add these features.

## Q: How many admins can there be with role-based access to manage the analytics dashboard?

**A:** The number of user accounts with access to the analytics dashboard will depend on your selected plan. For the SME plan, up to 2 user accounts can be granted access, while the Pro plan allows for up to 5 user accounts with dashboard management permissions.
