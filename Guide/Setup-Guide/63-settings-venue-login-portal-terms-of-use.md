# 6.3 Settings - Venue & Login portal - Terms of Use


Within the Captive Portal, FansWiFi offers a default template of Terms and Conditions that are embedded for your convenience. These pre-set Terms and Conditions aim to safeguard the company or organization's needs and best interests.

However, you have the flexibility to customize and modify these Terms and Conditions to align them with specific requirements and policies. This customization ensures that the Terms and Conditions accurately reflect the organization's unique needs.

![](../../_images/63-settings-venue-login-portal-terms-of-use-423.png)

By modifying the template, ensures that the Terms and Conditions accurately reflect the company's practices, legal obligations, and any additional terms the company may want to include. This customization empowers you to address specific concerns, comply with local regulations, and establish clear guidelines for users accessing the Captive Portal.

![](../../_images/63-settings-venue-login-portal-terms-of-use-424.png)

Customizing the Terms and Conditions to suit the unique needs of the company serves to safeguard business interests and ensures that users are fully informed and consenting to the terms of using your network or services through the Captive Portal.
