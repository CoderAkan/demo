# Efficient Access Control and Custom Coupons for Merchants


Our centralized access control system allows you to manage access control settings for your locations, locally or globally. We also provide details on creating custom coupons based on your restaurant ads, as well as ensuring a unified experience for users across multiple branches.

## Centralized Access Control

![](../_images/efficient-access-control-and-custom-coupons-for-merchants-347.jpg)

Our system offers a centralized approach to access control management. As a company, you can assign your administration team to centrally manage access control settings for all your locations, whether they are spread locally or globally. This means you have the flexibility to control and monitor access from anywhere in the world, providing you with enhanced security and convenience.

## Location-Based Ads

![](../_images/efficient-access-control-and-custom-coupons-for-merchants-348.jpg)

With our system, you can easily set and control location-based ads for your restaurants. Whether you have branches in different cities or even different countries, our platform allows you to target specific ads to each location. This ensures that your promotions and marketing campaigns are optimized to reach the right audience in the right place, maximizing their effectiveness.

## Custom Coupons

![](../_images/efficient-access-control-and-custom-coupons-for-merchants-349.jpg)

To further enhance your promotional efforts, our system enables you to create custom coupons based on your restaurant ads. This means you can design targeted promotions tailored to specific branches or even individual locations. For example, if you have branches numbered from 001 to 088, you can easily create unique coupons for each branch, providing personalized offers to your customers.

## Unified Experience

![](../_images/efficient-access-control-and-custom-coupons-for-merchants-350.jpg)

We understand the importance of providing a seamless user experience across multiple branches. With our system, users can enjoy a unified login experience across all your restaurant locations. Whether they visit Branch 001, Branch 002, or any other branch, they can log in using the same credentials and access their personalized information and preferences. This eliminates the need for multiple accounts and ensures a convenient and consistent experience for your customers.
